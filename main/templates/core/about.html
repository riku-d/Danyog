About Danyog

Danyog is a charity platform that connects people who wish to donate their old items to orphanages and those in need. Our initiative spreads smiles across Kolkata and beyond.

We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Connecting donors with orphanages and individuals in need.
  • Providing essential items like food, clothing, and shelter to the less fortunate.
  • Promoting authenticity in charity work, ensuring that donations reach the right people.

Our Vision

At Danyog, we believe in creating a charity ecosystem that promotes kindness and support for those in need. We aim to reach out to individuals across Gujarat, focusing on various social issues including:

  • Women Empowerment
  • Poverty Alleviation
  • Medical Assistance
  • Support for Orphanages
  • And more...

Join Us in Making a Difference!

Your contributions, whether in kind or service, can have a significant impact on the lives of many. As we grow, we continue to seek out new ways to support our community and ensure that our efforts are felt where they are needed most.
